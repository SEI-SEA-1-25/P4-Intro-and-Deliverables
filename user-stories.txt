# Sceduling app allowing users to book an appointment for services with favorite barbers and 
barbers can track of their appointments


Client User will chose:
- the barber and add to favorite
- the service type
- date of appointment
- time of app


User Profile:
- user info (edit)
- all, upcoming and prev appointments
- cancel an appointment


-Tech: React, Typescript(front end), Node.js, Express, JS(backedn), MongoDB, bootstrap
