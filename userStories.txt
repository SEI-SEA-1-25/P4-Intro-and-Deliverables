User will be able to enter a search term, sign up, and/or login on the home page
If user clicks signup, they will be brought to another page to enter their email address and create a username and password. Once they fill that out, they will be brought to their newly formed profile page.
If user clicks login, they will be brought to another page to input their username and email address. After they successfully login, they will be brought to their profile page.
User will be able to search without signing up or in, they will not be permitted to comment on or save anything
User will be able to search for music by entering the artist's name or track name and the app will return information about the artist and/or track.
